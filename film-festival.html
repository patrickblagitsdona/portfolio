SWISS FILM FESTIVAL

The objective of this project was to design a graphic poster design concept for an imaginary swiss film festival. The goal was to create an homage illustration style with a modern touch.

 

The target audience for the Swiss Film Festival would be adults, who are interested in swiss cinematography.

 

Design challenges relating to the poster design for the Swiss Film Festival would be the homage to the vintage swiss poster designs from the 1970s and the implementation of substitution as a design technique.

 

To design the poster design for the Swiss Film Festival, Adobe Illustrator was the software of choice.

 

The outcome of the project was a success. The goal of creating a poster design for the Swiss Film Festival, through an homage to the vintage swiss posters from the 70s was reached. The colour choice was based on the colour scheme used on the vintage swiss posters. The typeface choice was based on the type choices on the swiss posters, which were mainly. All of the design choices and strategies were made to accomplish the homage design style.
