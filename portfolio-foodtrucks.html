Pre Press / Preflight

As the market for food truck wraps is usually at an all-time high during the summer months, there is a constant stream of new trucks that need wraps done. Due to the multiple trucks that came through MYC Media’s Foodtruckwraps.ca division, there are a lot of examples of different wraps to show. These selected few are worth showing due to their diversity and intricate designs.



Krankie Frankie's

This design involved a logo creation, as well as the design of the truck wrap. The client supplied a sketch of what they would like to be appropriated into the logo. Once the logo was created, that dictated the design for the background, colour scheme and design style; modern, clean, and simple, but at the same time eye-catching.

McEwan Catering

They are a well-established company in Toronto that wanted to be able to transport its kitchen to different locations. Therefore, the goal was to show that they cater all around the Greater Toronto Area (the GTA) and also showcase their brand through the wrap, so customers can still make the connection between McEwan foods and the McEwan Catering Food truck.
