About.me

My name is Patrick Blagits Dona, A Brazilian Graphic Designer [Provisional RGD] currently based in Orangeville, ON, Canada. Focused on Visual Identity, Fleet graphics and Editorial Design, but always looking forward to a challenge in different design fields.

Industry experience working with large corporations, creating visual identities, fleet graphics, interior and exterior signage. Graduate from a three-year advanced Graphic Design diploma program at Niagara College. I bring a unique perspective and diverse aesthetic with international work experience in Brazil, China and Canada. Additionally, I am fluent in English and Portuguese; I also speak casual Mandarin and Spanish.
