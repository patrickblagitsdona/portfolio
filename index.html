About.me

My name is Patrick Blagits Dona, I am a Brazilian Graphic Designer [student RGD] currently based in Orangeville, ON, Canada. Focused on Visual Identity, Packaging and Editorial Design, but always looking forward to a challenge in different design fields.

In April 2021, I will graduated from the Graphic Design program at Niagara College, in ON, Canada. I’ve had international work experience, in Brazil, China, and Canada. I am originally from São Paulo, Brazil, I’ve moved to Shenzhen, China, to finish high school and now Ontario, Canada. I am fluent in English and Portuguese, I also speak casual Mandarin and Spanish.
